The domain expertise, the methodological choices, and the quality control are entirely human. Claude Sonnet 4.6 acts as an AI copilot — writing the code, generating the charts, formatting the reports — all under the analyst's direction. This project shows how guided AI use can deliver professional-grade results at scale.
